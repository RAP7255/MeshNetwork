The Problem We're Solving

During natural disasters, power outages, or infrastructure collapse, traditional communication networks often fail. This leaves communities isolated without access to emergency services or coordination. How Our System Creates a Resilient Mesh

1

BLE Mesh Networking

Smartphones and dedicated hardware devices automatically form a decentralized mesh topology. Each device acts as a relay node, passing messages to extend the network's range.

2

One-Tap Software SOS

The mobile app provides a simple interface to broadcast an emergency alert to all reachable nodes in the network with a single tap.

3

Hardware SOS Button Integration

A dedicated, low-power BLE button allows for instant emergency alerts, ideal for high-stress situations or for users with limited mobility.

4

Store-and-Forward Logic

If a message cannot be delivered immediately, it's cached locally and automatically forwarded once a new connection is established, ensuring reliable message delivery even in disconnected environments.

Under the Hood: Our Core Technology

Secure Communication

All messages are encrypted using AES/RSA to ensure data privacy and authenticity, protecting sensitive information.

Flooding Mesh Propagation

A robust algorithm ensures every node that receives a message forwards it to its neighbors, guaranteeing full mesh coverage and rapid propagation.

Power-Efficient Design

The app uses low-energy BLE scanning intervals and background operation to preserve battery life, making it a reliable tool for extended periods.

Adaptive Hopping

Adaptive Frequency Hopping dynamically switches between 40 BLE channels to reduce the risk of jamming and ensure reliable signal transmission.

Real-World Resilience

No Network

The system uses its BLE mesh to relay messages offline, completely bypassing the need for cellular towers or Wi-Fi.

Power Blackout

Our solution is designed to operate on minimal power, allowing it to function for extended periods on a device's battery.

Partial Connectivity

Cached messages are auto-rerouted and forwarded when new nodes join the mesh, ensuring delivery even with intermittent connections.

High-Stress Situations

The dedicated hardware button enables users to trigger an emergency alert instantly, without needing to interact with their phone.
